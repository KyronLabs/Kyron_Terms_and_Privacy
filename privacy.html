Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 24, 2025

At Kyron (hosted at kyron.spidroid.com), we value your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we handle it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to run the service:

  • Account Info: Username, email address, and encrypted password.
  • Content: Posts, comments, and images you upload.
  • Logs: IP address, user-agent string, and timestamps for security and debugging.

2. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies strictly for:

  • Authentication: To keep you logged in during your session.
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand site performance.

No Third-Party Ads: We do not use third-party advertising trackers or sell your browsing history to data brokers.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain the Kyron platform.
  • Monitor for abuse, spam, and security threats.
  • Analyze aggregated usage trends to improve performance.

We do not sell your personal data.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

You may delete your account at any time via your user settings. Upon deletion:

  • Your public profile and content will be removed immediately.
  • Your data is permanently wiped from our active databases within 30 days.
  • Note: Certain server logs may be retained for a limited time strictly for legal or security auditing purposes.

5. Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

Kyron is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 13. If we learn we have collected such data, we will delete it immediately.
