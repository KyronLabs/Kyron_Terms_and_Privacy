What we collect & why

Transparency first

Account data (you give us)

  • Username – public identifier.
  • Email – used only for login, password resets, and important service notices. Never published.
  • Password – stored salted & hashed with bcrypt 12 rounds. We don't know it.
  • Optional profile fields: display name, bio, avatar, website, pronouns. All optional, all editable, all deletable.

Content data (you create)

  • Public posts, comments, likes, re-shares.
  • Uploads (images, videos, audio). We strip hidden EXIF/GPS data on upload.
  • Direct messages – encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We cannot read them.

Technical data (automatic)

  • IP address – kept 90 days for rate-limiting & abuse detection, then anonymised.
  • Device type, OS, browser – short-term server logs to debug crashes.
  • Referrer – temporary analytics to see where traffic comes from (no Google Analytics, we use Plausible, no cookies).

No-no list (what we never collect)

  • No real-name requirement.
  • No phone number.
  • No payment info (we don't charge yet).
  • No location tracking.
  • No third-party advertising cookies.

Cookies & local storage

We use two types:

Strictly necessary

Session cookie kyron_sid – keeps you logged in. HttpOnly, Secure, SameSite=Lax.

Preference

theme=dark – remembers your colour choice, stored in localStorage.

Third-party? Zero. No Facebook pixels, no Google AdSense, no "like" buttons.

How to revoke

Delete cookies in browser settings or click the "Log out everywhere" button in Settings → Security.

How we share (spoiler: we rarely do)

We are not a data broker. We share only:

Public content

Anyone can see your public posts via the web, RSS, or API (rate-limited).

Service providers

Our hosting provider (Hetzner) and e-mail relay (Mailgun) – both GDPR-compliant, bound by DPAs.

Legal requests

We scrutinise every subpoena or court order. Where allowed, we notify you so you can object.

Merger/acquisition

If Kyron is sold, you get 30-days notice and can delete your data before transfer.

We never sell, rent, or trade personal data.

Your choices & rights

GDPR, CCPA, LGPD

Access

Download everything we have about you in Settings → "Export data".

Correction

Edit profile any time.

Deletion

Delete account – irreversible, completed within 30 days.

Portability

Export gives you JSON/CSV you can take elsewhere.

Restrict processing

Contact us and we'll pause certain uses (except storage).

Object

You may object to any processing based on legitimate interest.

Submit requests via privacy@spidroid.com. We respond within 30 days. No discrimination: exercising your rights won't change your service.

Data retention & deletion

Category Retention Notes
Account profile Until deletion You control it.
Public posts Until deletion Removed from feeds instantly, CDN cache 24h.
Direct messages Until both sides delete Encrypted, can't be read by us.
IP logs 90 days Then anonymised.
Back-ups 30 days Rolling snapshots for disaster recovery.
What if I'm suspended, not deleted?

Data stays frozen during suspension so we can investigate. If you are re-instated, nothing is lost. If banned, data is wiped within 30 days unless we must keep it for legal evidence.

Security measures

TLS 1.3 everywhere (A+ on SSL Labs)
Passwords: bcrypt 12 + per-user salt
2-factor auth (TOTP) available
Database encrypted at rest (AES-256)
Annual penetration tests
SOC-2 Type II compliant data-centre

We notify you within 72h if a breach affects your personal data.

Children & COPPA

13+ only

Kyron is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from them. If we learn we have, we delete the account and all data within 24h. Parents may contact us to request deletion.

International transfers

Servers are in the EU (Germany). If you access from outside the EU your data is processed there under GDPR adequacy decisions. We never ship data to third countries without Standard Contractual Clauses or your explicit consent.

Changes to this policy

We'll email you for material changes and post a summary on the home feed. Changes take effect 30 days after posting. Archived copies are available on GitHub.
